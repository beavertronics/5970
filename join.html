Join Beavertronics!

  • When: We meet most Tuesdays and Thursdays, from 3:00pm to 5:00pm. Sometimes our hours are longer, but we have many members who can't stay the entire time, and that's fine.
  • Where: 13002 SW 2nd St, Beaverton OR 97005 (Beaverton High School). Our shop and meeting location is in the building across SW. Erickson Ave from the main BHS building. We recommend that you park in the parking lot by the tennis courts.
  • Cost: $0.00. It costs ZERO dollars to be on Team Beavertronics. You come ready to learn, we'll provide the rest.
  • Registration: If you would like, you can contact our lead coach, Dr. Heather Meiring, at heather_meiring@beaverton.k12.or.us. You can also just show up- we do not require registration for normal meetings.
  • Food: We almost always have snacks!

What do I need to know in order to join?

You don't need to know anything to join the team- We'll teach you everything you need to know! You don't even have to be good at Math!
